## Localization Options

| Option | Language posture | Advantage | Disadvantage | Cost/complexity | Choose when | Do not choose when |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| A | One primary language | Fastest execution and strongest voice consistency. | Slower international reach. | Low | One market is clearly highest value. | The buyer is split across multiple language markets. |
| B | Primary plus one translated derivative | Keeps execution focused while testing a second market. | Requires translation review and localized CTAs. | Medium | Founder can validate one second market without doubling workload. | Translation quality cannot be reviewed. |
| C | Multilingual from day one | Builds international surface area early. | High operational load and higher quality risk. | High | Product already sells in multiple language markets. | The offer, site, and support are not ready for those markets. |
