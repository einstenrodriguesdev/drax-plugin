This is the 90-day (quarterly) content strategy: an editorial mission, content pillars mapped to hub-and-spoke clusters, a named content mix, and a derived editorial calendar. The system completes this strategy before unattended daily posting starts. Content volume is derived from cadence x pillars over the quarter, never a fixed count. The plan may change, but changes are recorded here before the calendar is updated.

## Content Mix

Allocate planned pieces by purpose using the Hero-Hub-Hygiene model (default), not a fixed count:

- Hero (~10%): tentpole reach pieces (launches, big swings)
- Hub (~30%): recurring audience content (series, cadence)
- Hygiene (~60%): evergreen, search-intent pieces (durable SEO)

Adjust the ratio only when the founder's product, buyer, or language strategy demands it.
